About Noor Kids AI Academy

Empowering the next generation with AI literacy - one child at a time.

Our Mission

At Noor Kids AI Academy, we believe that AI is not just for software engineers in Silicon Valley - it is for everyone, including children. Our mission is to make AI education accessible, affordable, and fun for kids across Pakistan and the Urdu-speaking world.

We started this academy because we saw a gap: most AI courses are either too expensive, too theoretical, or too advanced for children. We designed our curriculum from scratch, with kids in mind - using free tools, simple language, and projects that excite young minds.

Our Teaching Philosophy

Our course follows a 90% practical + 10% theory format. This means your child spends most of each class actually creating, experimenting, and problem-solving with real AI tools - not just listening to lectures.

  • Every class ends with a small project
  • Individual and group projects for collaboration
  • Bilingual instruction (Urdu + English)
  • Live classes, not pre-recorded videos
  • Small class sizes for personal attention

Module Highlight: Prompt Magic

In this exciting class, students learn how to talk to AI like a pro. They discover that the right "prompt" can turn a vague idea into a stunning image, story, or solution. Using Bing Image Creator (powered by DALL-E), each student generates their first AI image - a robot in a Pakistani school - and learns how changing one word changes the entire result.

By the end of this class, students can confidently create AI images for any purpose - school projects, greeting cards, posters, or just for fun.

Module Highlight: AI Storybook

One of our most loved modules - students write a 4-page children's story using ChatGPT, then generate matching illustrations with AI image tools. The final storybook is assembled in Google Slides and shared with the class.

This module combines creativity, language skills, and AI tool mastery - producing a tangible project students are proud to show family and friends.

💡 Urdu Voice Tips

  • Tap the mic 🎤 and speak in Urdu (اردو) or Roman Urdu
  • For best Urdu voice output, install Urdu TTS voice in your device settings (Accessibility > Text-to-Speech)
  • If Urdu voice is not installed, agent uses Hindi voice as fallback (similar sounds)
  • On Android phones, Urdu/Hindi voice usually works automatically
  • Tap the speaker icon 🔊 to mute/unmute voice replies
